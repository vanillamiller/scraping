Technical Specifications

Look inside the BlendJet® One - The World's Most Powerful Portable Blender®

  • Blender

    BlendJet One's powerful 5V electrical motor spins its 6-point stainless steel blades at a blazingly fast 22,000 RPM.

  • Battery

    The 2000mAh rechargeable battery makes 8-12 cups per charge. You can recharge via any USB port for 1-3 hours between uses.

  • Design

    BlendJet's compact and portable design is made from BPA free food-safe materials. It fits in most standard size cup holders. The built-in strainer can filter out excess pulp.

  • Safety

    Built-in safety features prevent the motor from overheating and the blades from spinning when the jar isn't connected to the base.
