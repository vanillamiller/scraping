Win the battle against clutter

Gather cuts the clutter of small desk items, acts as a central home base for your most used items, and keeps everything neatly in place.

Use it your way

Gather adapts to your workspace and workflow to make sure the tools you need are always within reach and is flexible enough to use in any space.

Configure

Move the pieces in hundreds of configurations to exactly the way you want to use it.

Grid System

Each piece can be rotated or positioned anywhere along the base. The grid system keeps everything perfectly aligned.

Magnets

Magnets embedded in the base allow you to attach additional pieces or even combine two full sets.

Spend more time creating

Eliminate the time you spend digging through a messy drawer to find things. Gather keeps your essentials always within reach.
